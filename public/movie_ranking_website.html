The Best Movies Recommended by Izzy

Izzy's Top Movies

The Departed (2006)

A masterclass in tension, deception, and moral ambiguity, The Departed distills Scorsese’s crime storytelling into a razor-sharp thriller.
Its layered performances, relentless pacing, and exploration of identity and loyalty make it one of the most electrifying modern crime films ever made.

Casino

More than a gangster movie, Casino is an epic autopsy of power, greed, and excess. Scorsese’s meticulous direction, operatic style, and
unflinching portrayal of corruption turn Las Vegas into a character itself, creating a visually stunning and thematically ruthless portrait
of rise and collapse.

The Godfather II (1974)

Widely regarded as the greatest sequel ever made, The Godfather Part II deepens the original by juxtaposing legacy and isolation. Its parallel
narratives, moral complexity, and tragic transformation of Michael Corleone elevate it beyond crime cinema into timeless, Shakespearean storytelling.
